I'm Pratyush Dadhich - a Spatial Designer and XR Builder based in Italy. When I’m not building immersive tools and systems, I’m out with a camera, looking for the same thing: how light defines space, and what a moment looks like from the inside.

This archive is the slower half of that practice. No brief, no deadline - just landscapes, strangers, and the occasional perfect hour of light. Whether it’s the quiet of the Dolomiti or the energy of a city street, my goal is to record something that resonates before you can name why.

"I build spaces for a living. I photograph the ones I can't."
